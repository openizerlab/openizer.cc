EXPERIENCE

  • Bellastock festival

    Disco Soupe crew, Paris 2017-2019

    Supporting collective kitchens with funky beats + All Night Partying

  • Party Organizer

    Villette Makers, Paris w/ Animaux du Futur crew 2017

    We made cool blinking/lighting installations to welcome 200 peeps in the Fablab. Party kids had good fun in our swimming pool full of balls.

  • Euro Burner

    Nowhere festival w/ Garden of Joy camp 2013

    Detroit + Raving Techno sets + B2B with Jinto. We also set the T.E.M.P.L.E, a bizarre interactive installation.

  • US Burner

    Black Rock City w/ Bassenge camp 2012

    I wasn’t able to spinn, but attending Burning Man was brilliant. I highly recommend it to all human creatures.
